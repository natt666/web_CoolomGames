New Chapter: Dating Killmulator

Published: December 10, 2025 | By Coolom Team

The wait is over! We are thrilled to announce the release of the latest chapter of Dating Killmulator.

Romance Can Be Deadly

In this new update, we've pushed the boundaries of our mechanics. The choices you make now carry even more weight. Remember: in this game, a wrong look or a bad gift doesn't just end the date—it might end your life.

We have introduced two new characters and a new environment that many of you requested. We won't spoil the surprise, but let's just say that the "romantic dinner" scene turned out to be much bloodier than anticipated during testing.

Download the latest build on our Itch.io page and let us know if you survived your date!
