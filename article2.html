Behind the Scenes: Episode 13

Published: December 10, 2025 | By Coolom Team

VISIT OFFICIAL WEBSITE ➜

We usually have mixed feelings when we launch a new episode, but with Episode 13, we are actually really proud of how it turned out. However, this episode was supposed to be completely different.

From Mall Date to Funeral

Originally, the plan for this update was simple: Abel and Florian go to the mall for a cute date, ending on a high note with a trip to Paris. But then, life happened.

Instead of pausing everything, we found ourselves writing the episode while in the midst of a real funeral. It became a coping mechanism. We realized that some of the surreal, "fake" interactions you see at funerals would fit perfectly into the chaotic world of Dating Killmulator. So, we moved the funeral plotline forward and made it the center of the episode.

What's New in Ep 13?

This update is packed with unique details:

  • Two Different Intros: Depending on whether you killed Caleb in the past or not, the episode starts differently (though the outcome remains the same... for now).
  • Surreal Humor: Look out for Abel’s face shining like a Renaissance painting with a literal "God ray." It makes no sense, and we love it.
  • "Giving Condolences" Minigame: We added a new mechanic where you have to choose how to give condolences to Cassandra. You can be respectful, awkward, or even try to flirt with her (please don't, you piece of sh*t).

We hope you enjoy this strange, funny, and personal episode. It was a wild ride to make, and we think it's one of our best yet.
