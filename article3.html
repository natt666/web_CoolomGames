Saló del Manga BCN Recap & Devlog

Published: November 28, 2025 | By Coolom Team

Last week we had the pleasure of attending the Saló del Manga in Barcelona, and what an experience it was!

Community & Feedback

Meeting you all in person was the highlight of the event. We showcased a demo of the upcoming chapter of our flagship game, and the feedback was invaluable. Seeing your reactions live—the laughs, the scares, and the confusion—gives us so much energy to keep developing.

Devlog: Next Steps

Based on what we saw at the Saló, we are tweaking the dialogue system to make it snappier. The latest chapter is about 80% done. We are currently polishing the final boss encounter (yes, there is a boss battle now!) and finalizing the soundtrack.

Stay tuned for more updates, and thank you to everyone who stopped by our booth!
