Dating Killmulator: Season 1 Finale & Future Roadmap

Published: November 28, 2025 | By Coolom Team

First of all, Dating Killmulator is ending this December once Chapter 14 is released.

Yes, this is sad.

No, you shouldn’t be sad about it, because it will be back at the end of March with Dating Killmulator Season 2.

What to Expect from Season 2

Season 1 has always been about building mystery and developing the characters, and we are so excited to finally start answering many of the questions you’ve all been wondering about. Expect some wild plot twists and really fun storylines.

Season 2 will also wrap up almost all the main arcs, and it’s expected to last about the same as Dating Killmulator 1. It will also introduce a TON of new and exciting content that we cannot wait to start sharing with you.

We’ll keep the spoilers of this content exclusively on Patreon, so if you’re interested, you can get your first month at a discount using the code “HOHOHO”.

Why is there a break?

Honestly, it just makes sense for the story, and for us. The team has been working so long and so hard that you can start to see the cracks in our mental health. It’s not like we’ll stop working for three months; it’s just that we won’t be talking about it as much.

This way, we can focus on preparing chapters in advance (something we didn’t do before), so if someone has a health issue we can actually pause production without you noticing, which wasn’t possible before.

Thank you so much for supporting the game. We CANNOT wait to see your reactions to the final chapter. It is truly something special.
