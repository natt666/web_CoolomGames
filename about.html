Who is Coolom Games

2021

The Beginning

We met, the three of us, in a vocational training program at UVic. United by our passion for games and creativity, we discovered we shared the same dream: creating games that matter.

2022

Coolom Games is Born

We officially founded Coolom Games and started developing our first small games. Midlander was one of our early projects, where we learned the ropes of game development and began to find our unique voice.

2024

Breaking Through

October 2024 marked a turning point: Dating Killmulator was released and became the most successful Catalan game on itch.io! By the end of the year, we won Ludi 2024 for Best Replayability with Patufet, and DeVuego 2024 for Best Free Game with Dating Killmulator. An unforgettable year!
