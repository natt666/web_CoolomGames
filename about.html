Who is Coolom Games

2020

The Beginning

Coolom Games was founded by a group of passionate indie developers with a vision: to create games that speak to underrepresented audiences in gaming. We started small, with big dreams and even bigger hearts.

2021

First Projects

We dove into our first game jams and prototype development. Learning, failing, and growing together as a team. Every mistake was a lesson, and every success fueled our passion.

2022

Dating Killmulator

Our breakthrough moment! Dating Killmulator became the most successful Catalan game on itch.io. The response was overwhelming, and we knew we were onto something special.

2023

Awards & Recognition

Dating Killmulator won prestigious awards including Ludi, DeVuego, and an Extraordinary FP award. Our games started reaching audiences we never imagined, proving that diverse stories matter.

2024

Growing Forward

We continue to expand our portfolio with exciting new projects, always staying true to our mission: creating games with personality, creativity, and heart. The future is bright!
